# In-decoded

IndeCode is an experiment in making Indian public data more visual, interpretable, and citizen-centric. India produces vast amounts of information through censuses, elections, budgets, surveys, and administrative records, yet much of it remains inaccessible to ordinary citizens due to complex formats and fragmented sources. IndeCode explores how design, visualization, cartography, and storytelling can transform these datasets into intuitive experiences that reveal patterns, histories, and relationships hidden within the numbers. By reimagining public data as something to be explored rather than merely consumed, the project aims to help people better understand the country they live in and encourage a more informed public discourse.

*Decoding India, One Dataset at a Time.*
